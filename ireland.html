Study in Ireland

English-speaking EU nation, tech & pharma hub, with a 2-year stay-back option

Home / Destinations / Ireland

Why Study in Ireland?

Ireland is an English-speaking member of the EU and the European HQ for global giants like Google, Apple, Meta, and Pfizer — making it ideal for tech, business, and pharma students. Graduates can stay under the Third Level Graduate Programme (Stamp 1G) for up to 2 years (master's level) to find work.

📝
Visa Type
Long Stay (D) Study Visa
🎓
Main Intakes
September & January
⏱️
Processing Time
4–8 weeks
💼
Work Rights
20 hrs/week + Stamp 1G

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Academic transcripts & certificates
  • IELTS / approved English test (typically 6.0–6.5)
  • Letter of Acceptance from an Irish institution
  • Proof of tuition fee payment
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Proof of funds for living costs (per year)
  • Private medical insurance
  • Online visa application (AVATS) summary
  • Two passport-sized photos
  • Gap explanation / work experience (if applicable)

Admission Requirements

  • HSC for undergraduate; Bachelor's for postgraduate
  • IELTS 6.0–6.5 overall
  • Relevant academic background
  • Clear academic and financial documentation
  • Evidence of paid tuition fees before visa filing
💡 Tip: Pay tuition before applying for the visa — Irish visa officers expect proof of fee payment as strong evidence of genuine intent.

Step-by-Step Process & Timeline
