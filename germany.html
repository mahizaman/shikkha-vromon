Tuition-free public universities, engineering excellence, and a strong economy

Why Study in Germany?

Germany is famous for its tuition-free or low-fee public universities and world-leading engineering, automotive, and research programs. After graduation, international students can stay up to 18 months on a job-seeker residence permit to find work, with a clear pathway to long-term residence.

📝
Visa Type
National (D) Student Visa
🎓
Main Intakes
Winter (Oct), Summer (Apr)
⏱️
Processing Time
6–12 weeks
💼
Work Rights
120 full / 240 half days + 18-mo stay

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Academic transcripts & certificates (APS certificate may be required)
  • IELTS/TOEFL (English programs) or TestDaF/Goethe (German programs)
  • University Admission Letter (Zulassungsbescheid)
  • Statement of Purpose / Motivation Letter
  • CV/Resume
  • Blocked Account (Sperrkonto) proof of funds
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Visa application form & declaration
  • Biometric passport photos
  • Proof of accommodation

Admission Requirements

  • HSC + Studienkolleg (foundation) or qualifying year for undergraduate
  • Relevant Bachelor's for Master's programs
  • IELTS 6.0–6.5 for English-taught programs
  • German proficiency (B1–C1) for German-taught programs
  • Blocked account funded for one year of living costs
💡 Tip: A blocked account (Sperrkonto) is mandatory — you deposit one year's living expenses, released monthly after arrival. We help you set this up correctly. An APS certificate is required for Bangladeshi applicants.

Step-by-Step Process & Timeline

10–12 months before

APS & Language Prep

Obtain your APS certificate and prepare IELTS or German language tests.

7–9 months before

University Applications

Apply via uni-assist or directly to universities.

4–6 months before

Admission Letter

Receive your Zulassungsbescheid (admission letter).

3–4 months before

Blocked Account & Insurance

Open the blocked account and arrange health insurance.

2–3 months before

National (D) Visa

Apply at the German Embassy in Dhaka with all documents.

Departure

Fly to Germany

Register your address and convert to a residence permit after arrival.
