Study in Malta

Affordable, English-speaking EU education in the heart of the Mediterranean

Why Study in Malta?

Malta is a sunny, safe, English-speaking EU member state offering affordable, quality education. As a Schengen-area country, it gives students access to travel across Europe, a growing economy in IT, finance, and tourism, and part-time work rights during studies.

📝
Visa Type
National Long-Stay (D) Visa
🎓
Main Intakes
October & February
⏱️
Processing Time
4–8 weeks
💼
Work Rights
20 hrs/week (after 90 days)

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Academic transcripts & certificates
  • IELTS / approved English proficiency proof
  • Letter of Acceptance from a Maltese institution
  • Proof of tuition fee payment
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Proof of funds / bank statements
  • Health insurance covering Malta
  • Proof of accommodation in Malta
  • National (D) visa application form & photos
  • Police clearance certificate (if required)

Admission Requirements

  • HSC for undergraduate; Bachelor's for postgraduate
  • IELTS 6.0 (or institution-accepted equivalent)
  • Relevant academic background
  • Sufficient funds for tuition + living
  • Valid health insurance
💡 Tip: As a Schengen country, Malta requires comprehensive health insurance and proof of accommodation. Apply early — visa slots can be limited for popular intakes.
