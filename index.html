FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge for the initial consultation?
No. Your first consultation is completely free. We assess your profile and explain your options with no obligation.
Which IELTS/TOEFL score do I need?
It varies by country and university — typically IELTS 6.0–7.0 overall. Some countries like Germany and Japan also offer programs in their local language. We guide you on the exact requirement for your chosen program.
How much bank balance is required for a student visa?
Each country sets its own proof-of-funds requirement covering tuition + living costs (usually 1 year). We provide exact figures and help prepare your financial documents correctly.
Can I work while studying abroad?
Most of our destinations allow part-time work (typically 20 hours/week during term). Countries like the UK, Canada, Australia, and Ireland also offer post-study work visas.
How long does the whole process take?
From consultation to departure usually takes 3–8 months depending on the country and intake. We recommend starting at least 6–9 months before your intended intake.
