Why Study in Australia?

Australia combines top-ranked universities (several in the global top 100) with one of the world's highest standards of living. International graduates can apply for the Temporary Graduate Visa (Subclass 485) to live and work after their studies. All applicants must meet the Genuine Student (GS) requirement.

📝
Visa Type
Student Visa (Subclass 500)
🎓
Main Intakes
February & July
⏱️
Processing Time
4–12 weeks
💼
Work Rights
48 hrs/fortnight + Subclass 485

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Academic transcripts & certificates
  • IELTS / PTE / TOEFL scores
  • Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) from the institution
  • Genuine Student (GS) statement
  • Statement of Purpose & study plan
  • Proof of funds (tuition + living + travel)
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)
  • CV and work experience (if applicable)
  • Health examination & police clearance (if required)

Admission Requirements

  • HSC for undergraduate; Bachelor's for postgraduate
  • IELTS 6.0–6.5 (higher for some courses)
  • Genuine Student (GS) requirement satisfied
  • Evidence of sufficient funds
  • OSHC health insurance arranged
💡 Tip: The Genuine Student (GS) requirement replaced the old GTE. Australia closely assesses your study intent and finances — a well-prepared GS statement is critical.
