Why Study in the USA?

The United States hosts more top-100 universities than any other country and offers an enormous range of programs, world-class research facilities, and the flexible "liberal arts" system that lets you explore before specializing. International students can also benefit from Optional Practical Training (OPT) — up to 12 months of work after graduation, extended to 36 months for STEM degrees.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Academic transcripts & certificates (SSC, HSC, Bachelor's)
  • Standardized test scores — TOEFL/IELTS/Duolingo; SAT/GRE/GMAT (program-dependent)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP) / Personal Essay
  • Letters of Recommendation (2–3)
  • Updated CV/Resume
  • Form I-20 (issued by the university after admission)
  • SEVIS fee (I-901) payment receipt
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • Proof of funds — bank statements, sponsor affidavit, scholarship letters
  • Visa appointment & interview confirmation

Admission Requirements

  • Completed prior education (HSC for undergrad; Bachelor's for Master's)
  • Competitive GPA — varies by university ranking
  • English proficiency: typically IELTS 6.0–7.5 or TOEFL 80–100
  • GRE/GMAT for many graduate programs (waivers exist)
  • Strong SOP and recommendations
  • Proof of sufficient funds for tuition + living
💡 Tip: US applications are holistic — essays, extracurriculars, and recommendations matter as much as grades. Start 9–12 months before your intake.

Step-by-Step Process & Timeline

12 months before

Profile Assessment & Test Prep

Shortlist universities and prepare for TOEFL/IELTS and SAT/GRE/GMAT.

8–10 months before

Application Submission

Submit applications with SOP, LORs, transcripts, and test scores.

5–7 months before

Admission & I-20

Receive admission, accept the offer, and the university issues your Form I-20.

3–4 months before

SEVIS Fee & DS-160

Pay the SEVIS (I-901) fee and complete the DS-160 visa application.

2–3 months before

Visa Interview

Attend the F-1 interview at the US Embassy in Dhaka with all documents.

Departure

Fly to the USA

Pre-departure briefing, accommodation, and you can enter up to 30 days before your program start.
