Why Study in Japan?

Japan is a global leader in technology, engineering, and research, offering a safe society, rich culture, and many English-taught degree programs. Generous scholarships like MEXT and JASSO are available, and graduates can switch to a work visa to build a career in one of the world's largest economies.

📝
Visa Type
Student Visa (via COE)
🎓
Main Intakes
April & October
⏱️
Processing Time
2–3 months
💼
Work Rights
28 hrs/week (with permission)

Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Academic transcripts & certificates
  • IELTS/TOEFL (English programs) or JLPT (Japanese programs)
  • Letter of Acceptance from the institution
  • Certificate of Eligibility (COE) issued by Japan Immigration
  • Statement of Purpose / study plan
  • Proof of funds & sponsor documents
  • Bank statements & sponsor's income proof
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Visa application form
  • Health / medical documents (if required)

Admission Requirements

  • HSC for undergraduate; Bachelor's for postgraduate
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) or JLPT N2–N1 for Japanese programs
  • Relevant academic background
  • Clear financial sponsorship documentation
  • Strong, genuine study plan
💡 Tip: The key step is the Certificate of Eligibility (COE) — your institution applies for it on your behalf with Japanese immigration. Once issued, the actual visa stamping at the embassy is quick. Apply early for MEXT scholarships.

Step-by-Step Process & Timeline

8–10 months before

Shortlist & Language Test

Select institutions and complete IELTS/TOEFL or JLPT.

6–7 months before

Apply & Get Acceptance

Submit applications and receive your Letter of Acceptance.

4–5 months before

COE Application

Institution applies for your Certificate of Eligibility.

2–3 months before

Visa Application

With the COE, apply for the student visa at the Japanese Embassy in Dhaka.

Departure

Fly to Japan

Receive your Residence Card on arrival and register at the local ward office.
