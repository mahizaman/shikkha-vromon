Why Study in Malaysia?

Malaysia is a fast-growing education hub offering internationally recognized degrees at an affordable cost. It hosts branch campuses of world universities (such as Monash, Nottingham, and Heriot-Watt), plus the option of "twinning" and dual-award programs. English is widely used as the medium of instruction.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (12+ months validity)
  • Academic transcripts & certificates
  • IELTS / English proficiency (program-dependent)
  • Offer Letter from the Malaysian institution
  • EMGS Visa Approval Letter (VAL)
  • Completed health declaration form
  • Passport-sized photographs (white background)
  • Proof of funds / bank statement
  • Statement of Purpose (if required)
  • No-objection / gap explanation (if applicable)

Admission Requirements

  • HSC for undergraduate; Bachelor's for postgraduate
  • IELTS 5.5–6.5 (varies by program)
  • Relevant academic background
  • Health screening clearance
  • Sufficient funds for tuition + living
💡 Tip: Malaysian student visas are processed centrally through EMGS (Education Malaysia Global Services). You first receive a Visa Approval Letter (VAL), then enter and convert it into a Student Pass. We manage the EMGS process for you.
