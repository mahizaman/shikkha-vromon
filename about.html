Who We Are

Shikkha Vromon is a dedicated student consultancy based in Dhaka, committed to helping Bangladeshi students access world-class education across 9 countries. With experienced counselors and a network of trusted university partners, we simplify the complex journey of studying overseas — from the first consultation to your arrival abroad.

Our Mission

To empower students with honest guidance, expert resources, and full support so they can confidently pursue their academic dreams without barriers or confusion.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in student consultancy in Bangladesh — known for integrity, expertise, and an unwavering focus on student success.
